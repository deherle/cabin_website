Modern Luxury on the Lake

Lake Cowichan Vacation Rental

Welcome to our Lake Cowichan Vacation Home! Built in 2021, our home offers 2700 sq. ft. of modern luxury. We're currently renting the lower suite, which measures 1200 sq.ft. of interior living space across 2 bedrooms and 2 full bathrooms.

Our lower suite features an 800 sq. ft. composite deck, 7-person hot tub, 12' overhead door, (2) 5kW outdoor heaters, fire pit and BBQ.

Property Highlights

Fabulous Views

The suite and deck offer stunning views of Lake Cowichan and Bald Mountain. Steps from the beach and parks.

Beach and Park Access

The property backs onto Southern Playfield Park, great for kids! We're 200m from Stoker Park, offering a beach and swimming area.

Hiking and more

Hiking and biking trails abound just steps from our property. Access Bald Mountain and Bald Mountain Park 200m away.

Quiet Neighborhood

Woodland Shores is a serene, placid environment to relax and enjoy lake and mountain views. Herds of Elk regularly peruse the area.

Our Place

Modern 2-Bedroom Suite

Enjoy all that Lake Cowichan and our modern suite has to offer - nestle next to the fire, enjoy our expansive deck, open the overhead door to the deck or just enjoy the sunset and mountain views.
